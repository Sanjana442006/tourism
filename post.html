Panambur Beach: A Perfect Sunrise

By Admin | 15 Nov 2025

Panambur Beach is one of Kudla’s most popular beaches. Known for its golden sands, thrilling water sports, and peaceful sunrise views, it is a favorite for both locals and tourists. Early mornings are perfect for jogging, photography, and enjoying the serenity of the sea.

Besides water sports, visitors can also explore nearby cafes, enjoy fresh seafood, and take camel rides along the shore. Festivals like Kite Festival and Beach Festival bring energy and colors to Panambur.
